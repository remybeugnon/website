Paper 1: When biodiversity supports biodiversity by modulating climate


Contact person: Remy Beugnon
Related BN topic: Limits of tree and forest resilience; Future soils; Open Lands

Link to the document

Abstract/Idea

Idea: Thinking on how vegetation diversity; by modulating microclimatic conditions and increasing heterogeneous (spatial and temporal) climatic conditions; would support a higher niche space for understory communities and seedlings. Therefore, vegetation diversity should reinforce biodiversity over time.
